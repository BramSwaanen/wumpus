Instructions

The game you are about to play is set in a mine and you are the miner looking for gold. But beware: there is an ugly, dangerous Wumpus hiding in the cave.
Also, there are deadly pits. Your goal is to obtain a piece of gold without stepping onto a square where a Wumpus hides or where a pit is.
Luckily, you have the tools of one arrow and your impeccable reasoning skills. You get hints as to where the pits
and Wumpus may be: whenever the Wumpus is in a square directly adjacent to yours (horizontally and vertically, not diagonally),
you sense a 'stench'; whenever a pit is in a square directly adjacent to yours (also not diagonally) you observe a 'breeze'.
Whenever you kill the Wumpus, it disappears and you immediately observe the square where it was without having to walk onto it.
The controls are as follows:
- Up/down/left/right keys for movement;
- Space bar to fire an arrow;
- Enter to obtain gold.

The objective of the game is to obtain as many points as possible. Points are counted as follows:
- A move is -10 points;
- Shooting the arrow is -30 points;
- Obtaining the gold is +1000 points;
- Being killed by the wumpus or a pit is -1000 points;

Really try to get as many points as possible.

Whenever you step onto a square with a pit or Wumpus you're game over. Whenever you obtain the gold you win. In both cases you will be directed
to the next level.

You will play the game for different configurations of the world and we aks to explain your thought process as explicitly as possible.
Try to explain every thought step you make to the camera while playing the game. Whenever you are game over or obtain the gold you will
automatically be directed to the next level if you press "Next Level" in the pop-up message. Please turn of your computer's sound as well.

You will first get to play a practice level. After that you will be directed to the 'real' experiment, which consists of 3 levels.

Have fun!
